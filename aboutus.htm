The Radio Operators Of South Texas Amateur Radio Club (ROOST), A Texas Chartered 501c3 non profit organization, is located in southeast San Antonio Bexar County, Texas. ROOST is a club which is dedicated to the principles and good practice of amateur radio. Membership is open to all licensed amateurs. ROOST is active on all bands, most modes, chasing DX, special events, contests. Several of our members are active in various elements of Emergency Communications
