The Radio Operators of South Texas Amateur Radio Club (ROOST), A Texas Chartered 501c3 non profit organization, is located in southeast San Antonio Bexar County, Texas. ROOST is a club which is dedicated to the principles and good practice of amateur radio. Membership is open to all licensed amateurs. ROOST is active on all bands, most modes, chasing DX, special events, contests. Several of our members are active in various elements of Emergency Communications 
ARRL Amateur Radio Exam
ARRL VE Testing is provided the first and third Saturday of each month at the R.O.O.S.T. clubhouse at 7201 Palm Park Blvd. in San Antonio, [Click here for map and directions]. Testing begins at 1pm and the fee is $14.00. Walk-ins are welcome. Contact Chris or Dieter with any questions.
Club Information
The club meets at 7pm on the second Friday of every month at the R.O.O.S.T. clubhouse.  TheR.O.O.S.T information net meets every Thursday at 8pm on the 146.860 repeater. A 6 meter net meets every Monday evening at 8pm on 52.525 MHz (FM simplex). All nets are open to licensed amateurs.
